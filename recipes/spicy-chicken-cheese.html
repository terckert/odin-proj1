Spicy Chicken and Cheese Kenchiladas

Description

Keto-friendly take on chicken enchiladas without the tortillas.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp butter
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • 1 tsp taco seasoning mix
  • 1 8oz jar of salsa
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 4 oz fresh jalapeno, diced
  • 4 oz shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 4 ea green onion, sliced

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Over medium-high heat, saute chicken until juices run clear. Drain excess liquid. Add taco seasoning and toss to coat. Transfer chicken into baking dish coated in non-stick cooking spray.
  3. Mix salsa, sour cream and jalapenos together in a bowl and pour over the chicken.
  4. Bake until hot, about 25 minutes. Remove from oven and cover with cheddar cheese. Return to oven until cheese is fully melted.
  5. Let cool for 5-10 minutes before cutting into pieces and garnish with green onions.
